Site Name and Purpose

Site Name

Dojos Directory - Kickboxing & Muay Thai

Site Purpose

The Dojos Directory is a comprehensive online platform designed to connect martial arts enthusiasts with Kickboxing and Muay Thai training facilities worldwide. The site serves as a centralized directory where users can discover, compare, and explore dojos based on location, discipline, pricing, and ratings. It provides detailed information about each facility, including contact details, monthly pricing, discipline specialization, and user ratings, enabling prospective students to make informed decisions about their martial arts training journey.

Additionally, the platform allows dojo owners to register their facilities, expanding the directory and creating a growing community of martial arts practitioners and training centers.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Finding a Local Dojo

User: Maria, a 28-year-old professional who recently moved to a new city and wants to start training in Muay Thai.

Goal: Maria needs to find a Muay Thai dojo near her location that fits her budget and schedule. She visits the Dojos Directory homepage, sees featured dojos, then navigates to the complete directory page. She uses the filter buttons to show only "Muay Thai" dojos, browses through the results, and clicks on cards to view detailed information in the modal, including location, monthly price, rating, and description. She finds a dojo that matches her criteria and contacts them directly.

Scenario 2: Comparing Dojos and Registering

User: Carlos, a 35-year-old martial arts enthusiast who wants to compare different Kickboxing dojos before making a decision.

Goal: Carlos wants to compare prices, locations, and ratings of multiple Kickboxing dojos. He navigates to the directory page, filters by "Kickboxing" discipline, and views multiple dojo cards. He clicks on each card to see detailed information in the modal, comparing monthly prices, locations, and ratings. After finding the best option, he also notices the "Join" page and decides to register his own small dojo to expand the directory's reach.

Color Usage

  • Primary Black (#000000): Used for main text elements and strong contrast areas
  • Primary Red (#dc2626): Primary accent color for headings, buttons, and key interactive elements
  • Accent Red (#ef4444): Used for hover states, secondary accents, and highlights
  • Dark Red (#991b1b): Used in gradients and shadow effects for depth
  • Text White (#ffffff): Primary text color on dark backgrounds
  • Text Light (#f5f5f5): Secondary text color for better readability
  • Text Gray (#d4d4d4): Tertiary text color for less important information
  • Background Black (#0a0a0a): Main page background
  • Background Dark (#1a1a1a): Card and section backgrounds
  • Card Background (#1f1f1f): Individual card backgrounds
  • Border Dark (#333333): Borders and dividers

Font Usage

  • Headings: Arial Black/Bold with uppercase transformation and letter spacing for impact
  • Body Text: Arial for readability and consistency
  • Buttons & Navigation: Bold weight with uppercase and letter spacing for emphasis
  • Special Elements: Uppercase transformation with increased letter spacing for visual hierarchy

Google Fonts

This project uses system fonts (Arial family) for optimal performance and consistency across all devices. No external Google Fonts are imported, ensuring fast loading times and reliable rendering.
